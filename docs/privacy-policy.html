Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Antal.io ("we," "our," or "the Company") handles your information in relation to your use of the **AntalQR** mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the handling of information in accordance with this Policy.

1. Core Principle: Complete Privacy

AntalQR is designed to be an exclusively local data processing application.

We **do not collect, store, transmit, or disclose** any information or data generated by the user while using this App.

2. User Data Handling

2.1 QR Code Content

  • Input Data (Text, URL, etc.): The content you enter to generate the QR Code is processed **only within your device** and temporarily.
  • No Collection: The Company **does not have access** to this content. It is **not transmitted** to our servers or any third party.

2.2 Generated Images

  • Local Storage (Optional): The QR Code images you generate are saved **only** to your device's photo gallery, **if and only if** you explicitly select the "Save to Photos" function.

2.3 Usage Data and Analytics

AntalQR **does NOT use** analytics, tracking, or crash reporting tools that require an internet connection or the collection of usage data. Your use is **completely private and anonymous**.

3. Device Permissions

The App only requests one essential permission for its functionality:

  • Access to Photo Gallery (Photo Library): This permission is requested **only** when you decide to save a QR image. The App accesses this function only at your direct command.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy if we decide to add new features or services in the future. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
